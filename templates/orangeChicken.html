Orange chicken

Description

Orange chicken is a simple, but delicious recipe that will surprise any family member on a special occasion. We'll see all of The steps necessary to make this delicious dish step by step.

Ingredients

  • Vinegar
  • 3 Oranges
  • Soy sauce
  • Brown Sugar
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Corn starch
  • Garlic
  • Water
  • 1 chicken breast

Steps to prepare

For the sauce
  1. We put a cup of water to boil
  2. Add half a cup of vinegar, half a cup of orange juice and half a cup of soy sauce
  3. When it begins to boil add garlic and half a cup of brown sugar
  4. If you want a touch of spice add 2-3 dried chillies
  5. Add a pinch of corn starch to add consistency
For the chicken
  1. cover the chicken in seasoned flour
  2. cover in egg wash
  3. Fry chicken until well done
  4. Mix together with the orange sauce
  5. Enjoy!
